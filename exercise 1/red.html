Red Velvet Cake

Red Velvet Cake is a popular dessert with a rich, moist texture and a distinctive red color. It is traditionally layered with cream cheese frosting, which complements its subtle chocolate flavor.

The exact origin of Red Velvet Cake is debated, but it gained popularity in the United States during the 20th century. Its deep red color is typically achieved through the reaction of acidic vinegar and buttermilk with cocoa powder, though nowadays, red food coloring is often used.
